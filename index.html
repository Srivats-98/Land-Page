REENA'S STORY

OUR MISSION

Since 1959, The Association of People with Disability (APD) has been transforming the lives of differently-abled people, bringing them into the mainstream through tireless work and dedication. So far, we have reached out to over 33,000 people with disability. We are intent on reaching more, but this is an enormous challenge and our resources are limited.
